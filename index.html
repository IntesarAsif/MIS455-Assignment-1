About me

What I do

Sharingan

Sharingan grants the user enhanced visual perception, allowing them to see and analyze fast-moving objects, predict an opponent's movements, and perceive chakra.

Lighitning Blade

Chidori is a Lightning Release jutsu, which means it utilizes the user's chakra to create and control lightning. Lightning Release techniques are known for their speed and precision.

Kamuai

Kamui is notable for its ability to manipulate space, allowing the user to warp or transport objects to a different dimension. Kamui has the unique ability to transport objects, and later the user themselves, to another dimension.
